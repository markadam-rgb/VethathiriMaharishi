üå± About the Founder ‚Äì Yogiraj Vethathiri Maharishi

Yogiraj Vethathiri Maharishi was born on August 14, 1911, in Guduvancheri, Tamil Nadu, and passed away in 2006. He founded the World Community Service Centre (WCSC) in 1958 with the mission: "World Peace through Individual Peace."

He developed Vethathiriyam, a holistic science of living that integrates yoga, meditation, Kaya Kalpa rejuvenation, and introspection. Maharishi authored over 80 books and 2,000 philosophical poems in Tamil and English.
